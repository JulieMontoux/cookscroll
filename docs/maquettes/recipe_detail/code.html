Spicy Tuscan Pomodoro with Burrata

Prep 15m
Cook 25m
2
540 kcal
12g Prot
68g Carb
22g Fat
check
400g Spaghetti alla Chitarra Fresh or dried semolina pasta
check
200g Fresh Burrata Cheese Creamy center, served at room temp
check
2 tbsp Nduja or Chili Flakes For that signature Calabrian heat
check
Bunch of Thai Basil Torn roughly by hand
1

Bring a large pot of salted water to a rolling boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente (about 8-10 mins).

2

In a wide skillet, melt the Nduja with a splash of pasta water over medium heat until it emulsifies into a vibrant oil.

3

Toss the cooked pasta directly into the sauce. Add fresh tomatoes and basil, tossing vigorously to coat every strand.
